Mobile Deterrence

A static guard covers one point. A patrol covers everything. Our mobile patrol units move continuously across your designated area — checking perimeters, verifying access points, and maintaining a visible security presence that actively deters criminal activity.

Whether on foot, bicycle, or motorized vehicle, Uwezo Security patrol teams operate on randomized schedules to eliminate predictability and maximize deterrence across Kampala and Uganda.
