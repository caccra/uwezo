About Uwezo Security

Uganda's premier security services provider, delivering professional protection solutions with integrity, vigilance, and unwavering commitment to safety.

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Uwezo Security Services Limited was established to address the growing need for professional, reliable security services in Uganda. The name "Uwezo" comes from Swahili, meaning "capability" or "ability" - a principle that defines everything we do.

From our headquarters in Kampala, we've grown to become one of Uganda's most trusted security providers, serving residential, corporate, and industrial clients across the country. Our team of over 1,200 trained security professionals delivers round-the-clock protection with professionalism and dedication.
