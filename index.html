Who We Are

Protecting Uganda's People
Since 2011

Uwezo Security Services Limited was founded in Kampala with a simple belief — every Ugandan home, business, and organisation deserves to feel safe. "Uwezo" means capability in Swahili, and for over 15 years that is exactly what we have delivered. We are fully licensed by the Ministry of Internal Affairs and trusted by banks, NGOs, schools, businesses, and families across Uganda.

We take the time to understand your situation before recommending anything. Our guards are trained, vetted, and supported — because the quality of our people is the foundation of everything we do.
